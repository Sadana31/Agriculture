Problem Statement

The overuse of pesticides and fertilizers in agriculture has become a significant environmental concern. Excessive application of these chemicals can lead to water pollution, soil degradation, biodiversity loss, and adverse health effects.

This website aims to raise awareness about the issue and provide potential solutions to reduce the overuse of pesticides and fertilizers in farming practices.

Benefits

By reducing the reliance on pesticides and fertilizers, we can:

  • Protect water resources from contamination
  • Preserve soil quality and fertility
  • Maintain biodiversity and ecosystem health
  • Enhance human health and food safety

Proposed Solution

Implementing sustainable agricultural practices is key to addressing the overuse of pesticides and fertilizers. This includes:

  • Encouraging organic farming methods
  • Promoting integrated pest management (IPM)
  • Investing in research for alternative pest and weed control methods
  • Educating farmers about proper application techniques and dosage
